Privacy Policy

We built Chore Quest to be safe for the whole family — including kids. Read below to see exactly what we do (and don't) do with your data.

Effective Date: May 2, 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 2, 2026

🛡️ The Short Version

  • We do not collect any personal information from you or your children.
  • All app data is stored only on your device — nothing is sent to any server.

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Chore Quest ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our application. Chore Quest is a family chore-tracking tool designed to help parents assign household tasks to children, who earn virtual ticket rewards for completing them.

This policy applies to the Chore Quest web application hosted at https://GitHubNomad.github.io/chore-tracker, the installable Progressive Web App (PWA) version, and any version distributed through the Google Play Store.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you are a parent or guardian installing this App for your child, you accept this policy on behalf of your family.

2. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. The App does not ask for, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other contact details
  • Account credentials or passwords (there are no accounts)
  • Device identifiers such as IMEI, serial number, MAC address, or advertising ID
  • Location data (precise or approximate)
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Biometric data of any kind
  • Payment or financial information

What the App Stores Locally

The App uses your device's browser localStorage (a standard web technology) to save your family's progress between sessions. This data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted anywhere. It includes:

  • The list of chores and their completion status
  • Virtual ticket balances (earned and redeemed)
  • Daily completion history used to calculate streaks
  • Achievement badges earned
  • Custom chores added by a parent
  • The parent PIN (stored locally on-device only)
All of this data exists only on your device. Clearing your browser data, reinstalling the app, or switching devices will erase it. We have no way to recover it because we never had access to it in the first place.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Chore Quest is a family tool. While children use the App to track chores and earn rewards, the App is set up and managed by a parent or guardian. No child-specific information is collected or processed.

Our Commitment

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area).
  • The App does not include registration, accounts, or any mechanism by which a child could submit personal information.
  • The App does not display advertisements to children.
  • The App does not contain social features, chat functionality, or any means of contacting strangers.
  • No behavioral profiling or tracking of children occurs.

Parental Control

Parents access administrative features — including resetting all data — through the Parent Settings screen, which is protected by a PIN. Parents can delete all locally stored data at any time by using the "Reset All Progress" function in Parent Settings, or by clearing the app's data through their device's browser or app settings.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from or about a child without proper consent, please contact us immediately at the address below and we will take prompt corrective action.

8. Data Security

Because all App data is stored locally on your device using standard browser localStorage, the security of that data is governed by your device's security controls (screen lock, biometric authentication, device encryption, etc.).

We recommend:

  • Using a strong PIN or biometric lock on your device
  • Setting a Parent Settings PIN within the App to prevent children from accessing administrative functions
  • Not using the App on shared or public devices where others may access local storage

Since no data is transmitted to any server by us, there is no server-side data breach risk associated with the App. We cannot access, recover, or restore any App data.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

All App data is stored only for as long as it remains on your device. You have complete control over your data at all times.

How to Delete Your Data

  • In-App: Open the Parent Settings screen (🔐 tab) and use "Reset All Progress" to erase all chore history, tickets, streaks, and achievements.
  • Browser (web version): Open your browser settings → Site Settings → Clear data for this site.
  • Android (Play Store version): Go to Device Settings → Apps → Chore Quest → Storage → Clear Data.

Deletion is immediate and permanent. Because we hold no copy of your data on any server, there is nothing further for us to delete on our end.
